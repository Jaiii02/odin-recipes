Lasagna

  1. Ingredients :

    • For the meat sauce :

      • 2 tbs olive oil
      • 1 onion, finely chopped
      • 3 cloves garlic, minced
      • 500g ground beef
      • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
      • 2 tbsp tomato paste
      • 1 tbsp dried oregano
      • 1 tbsp dried basil
      • salt and pepper to taste
      • 1 tbsp sugar

    • For the Bechemel Sauce :

      • tbsp butter
      • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
      • 2 cups (500ml) milk
      • Salt and pepper to taste
      • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    • For the assembly :

      • 9–12 lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or no-boil)
      • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
      • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  2. Steps :

    1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
    2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
    3. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
    4. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
    5. Simmer on low heat for 20–30 minutes until thickened. Stir occasionally.
    6. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and whisk for 1–2 minutes to make a roux.
    7. Slowly whisk in the milk, making sure no lumps form.
    8. Simmer until the sauce thickens (5–7 minutes). Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and stir in Parmesan.
    9. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
    10. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
    11. Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
    12. Spread a layer of meat sauce, then béchamel, and sprinkle some mozzarella.
    13. Repeat layers: pasta → meat sauce → béchamel → mozzarella, ending with béchamel and mozzarella on top.
    14. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.
    15. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    16. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15–20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
    17. Let rest for 10–15 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
