Pizza

  1. Ingredients :

    • Pizza base
    • Pizza Sauce
    • Cheese
    • Chopped onions
    • Chopped Capsicum
    • Chopped Tomato

  2. Steps :

    1. Apply the sauce evenly on the pizza base.
    2. Evenly spread out the chopped veggies on the pizza base.
    3. Evely spread out the cheese on top.
    4. Microwave for about 10 minutes.
